ABOUT

Since 1997 we have been doing fabrication, erections, maintenance and structural business
in the name of AISHWARYA ENGINEERING WORKS committed to being a service provider to
clients throughout the region. Today, we are operating from our original location offering all
kind of structural, pipeline fabrication, erection, maintenance works as well as man power
supply in all industries, with a workforce of over 50, our experienced team is embedded
with good skills and abilities enabling us to meet any kind of challenge in our field. We have
the capability, facility and equipment to do small, medium and large tasks in any of the
industries we work within. We undertake projects in a manner that provides value to our
clients. to reach our objective we plan, implement and improve our services, skill and
competencies of our workforce. We respond very quickly to the needs of our clients. We
promote loyalty and commitment amongst all our employees for our clients. Our first
commitment is to provide best services on time to our clients.

OUR KEYS TO SUCCESS

1- Providing good quality service on time and on low budget to our clients. 2- Working honestly and developing strategy to gauge performance and build trust with our clients. 3- Working closely with our customers and communicating with transparency.
